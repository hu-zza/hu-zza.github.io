permalink


Permalinks are static, never-ever changing pointers to some resource.

At zza.hu each of them starts with perma/ prefix, so it's easy to spot one:
https://zza.hu/perma/Java_SE_11_PriorityQueue

This points to:
https://github.com/AdoptOpenJDK/openjdk-jdk11/blob/19fb8f93c59dfd791f62d41f332db9e306bc1422/src/java.base/share/classes/java/util/PriorityQueue.java

Which is also a permalink. (But harder to remember.)

You can also find template links on zza.hu.
