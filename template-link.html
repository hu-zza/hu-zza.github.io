template link


Template links are dynamic, versatile pointers to some resource.

At zza.hu each of them starts with either * or _ prefix. These two are equivalent:
https://zza.hu/*Java_11_src/java.base/java/util/PriorityQueue.java
https://zza.hu/_Java_11_src/java.base/java/util/PriorityQueue.java

... and point1 to:
https://github.com/AdoptOpenJDK/openjdk-jdk11/blob/master/src/java.base/share/classes/java/util/PriorityQueue.java


Without java/util/PriorityQueue.java these point to the java.base module2, while https://zza.hu/*Java_11_src and https://zza.hu/_Java_11_src point to the repository2.

https://zza.hu/*Java_11_src/java.base
https://github.com/AdoptOpenJDK/openjdk-jdk11/tree/master/src/java.base/share/classes/

https://zza.hu/*Java_11_src
https://github.com/AdoptOpenJDK/openjdk-jdk11/tree/master/src/


1: In reality these point to https://github.com/AdoptOpenJDK/openjdk-jdk11/tree/master/...
2: The arbitrary selection of a subfolder (in these cases /share/classes/ and /tree/master/src/) is maybe distracting first, but I try to compose these templates with the most frequent usage in my mind.

You can also find permanent links (permalinks) on zza.hu.
